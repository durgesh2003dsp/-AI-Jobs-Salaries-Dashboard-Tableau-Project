# AI-Jobs-Salaries-Dashboard-Tableau-Project
This Tableau project analyzes global AI job salaries, employment types, experience levels, and job roles across multiple countries. The dashboard gives a complete view of salary variations, workforce distribution, and AI job market trends using interactive visualizations such as maps, donut charts, scatter plots, and tables.

## **1. Project Overview**

This Tableau project analyzes global AI job salaries, employment types, experience levels, and job roles across multiple countries.
The dashboard gives a complete view of salary variations, workforce distribution, and AI job market trends using interactive visualizations such as maps, donut charts, scatter plots, and tables.

## **2. Key Objectives**

Compare AI salaries across countries and identify high-paying regions.

Analyze salary variations by experience level, job title, and employment type.

Visualize the distribution of employees across different countries.

Provide insights into company size and employment structure in the AI sector.

Build an interactive dashboard for quick filtering and exploration.

## **5. Insights & Takeaways**

USA, Canada, and Western Europe offer the highest salaries in AI.

Salary strongly increases with experience level.

Majority of roles are full-time, with contract work being minimal.

France, Germany, and Denmark are top countries for employee distribution.

Expert-level roles in AI regularly exceed $160K-$175K.
